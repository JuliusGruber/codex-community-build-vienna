# Export My Stats — what it feels like to use

**The problem it solves.** All your AI-coding stats live inside the Coach app — your totals, how much code came from the AI versus you, your top languages, your flow score, your most common anti-patterns. They look great on screen, but they're trapped there. If you want to drop a snapshot into your standup notes, share your progress with a mentor, keep a record from month to month, or feed the numbers into something else, you'd be stuck squinting at the screen and retyping.

**The feature.** A small **Export** button sits in the corner of the app. Click it and you're offered a few choices: **Download as Markdown**, **Download as JSON**, or **Copy Markdown** to your clipboard. Pick one and you instantly get a clean, ready-to-share summary of your stats — the Markdown version is nicely formatted for reading and pasting into docs or chat, and the JSON version is the raw numbers for anyone who wants to work with the data. Downloaded files come with the date in the name, so snapshots don't overwrite each other.

**Why it's nice.**

- **Shareable.** Hand your progress to a teammate, mentor, or manager without screenshots.
- **Portable.** Paste the Markdown straight into standup notes, a journal, a PR description, or a chat.
- **A record over time.** Each dated export is a snapshot, so you can look back and see how your habits changed.
- **Two audiences, one click.** Markdown for humans, JSON for tools — your choice at the moment you export.

**A quick scenario.** It's the end of the sprint and you want to show how your AI usage went. You click **Export**, choose **Copy Markdown**, and paste a tidy summary — totals, top languages, flow score, the anti-patterns you're working on — straight into your team channel. No retyping, no screenshots, and you've got a dated file saved for your own records too.

It's the familiar "Export" or "Download report" you've met in analytics dashboards and finance apps — one button that turns what's on screen into something you can take with you.
